MICHELANGELO

Michelangelo di Lodovico Buonarroti Simoni was a sculptor, painter, and architect from the Renaissance.

Well known especially for his sculptures, Pieta (seen to the right), David, he is also famous for painting the roof of the Sistine Chapel.
