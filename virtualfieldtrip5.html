BOTTICELLI

Born on March 1, 1445, in Florence, Italy, and died on May 17, 1510, in Florence Italy, Alessandro di Mariano di Vanni Filipepi (nicknamed Botticelli, little barrel, by his brother giovanni) was a master painter who painted both religious and mythological scenes.

As a boy, Botticelli apprenticed as a goldsmmith and then with master painter Filippo Lippi. He's well known for his work on the Sistine Chapel, as well as his paintings Primavera and The Birth of Venus.
