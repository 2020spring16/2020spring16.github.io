Botticelli

Alessandro di Mariano di Vanni Filipepi (nicknamed Botticelli, little barrel, by his brother giovanni) was a master painter who painted both religious and mythological scenes.

As a boy, Botticelli apprenticed as a goldsmmith and then with master painter Filippo Lippi. He's well lknown for his work on the Sistine Chapel, as well as his paintings Primavera and The Birth of Venus.
